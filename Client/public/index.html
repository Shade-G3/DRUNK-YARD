The Philosophy of the Yard

In a world of "likes," algorithms, and perfectly curated feeds, we lost something vital: The Unfiltered Night. DRUNKYARD is a digital tavern — the virtual version of those 2 AM conversations where ego disappears, guards come down, and truth comes out.

Why We're Here

  • Zero Polish, All Soul: We care about what you have to say right now, not your profile picture.
  • Real-Time, Real Human: Powered by WebRTC — no delays, no "seen" receipts, just raw dialogue.
  • The "Backyard" Vibe: Ideas too wild for LinkedIn, too honest for Instagram.

The Yard Rules

  • Be Authentic: Bring your realest self.
  • Respect the Space: "Drunk" honesty, not "drunk" hostility.
  • Leave it on the Floor: What happens in the Yard stays in the Yard.
